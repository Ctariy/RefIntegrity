Source
  1. Enter a paper title or identifier — or upload a BibTeX/RIS file.
  2. Automatic cross-check — all references checked against Retraction Watch via OpenAlex.
  3. Instant results — flagged refs shown with status, date, and retraction notice link.

Unlike manual lookups, RefIntegrity checks an entire reference list in seconds.
